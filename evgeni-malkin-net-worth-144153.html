Evgeni Malkin Net Worth

Evgeni Malkin is a Russian NHL All-Star who was born on July 31, 1986 in Magnitogorsk, Russia. He has had a successful career in the NHL, helping the Pittsburgh Penguins win three Stanley Cup Championships in 2009, 2016 and 2017. He was also named NHL MVP in 2012 and won the Rookie of the Year award in 2007.
