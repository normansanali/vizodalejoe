What is Tritan crystal glass?

published on July 05, 2024
Answer Schott Zwiesel created Tritan® crystal glass, which is a one-of-a-kind, globally patented crystal glass that is used in a variety of applications. It is fully devoid of lead and barium, and instead makes use of titanium and zirconium oxides as pigments. It is manufactured using a high-temperature manufacturing method that involves tempering. Is Tritan crystal a safe material to work with? Unlike other types of glass, Tritan Crystal is completely dishwashing safe and will not etch, cloud, or discolour throughout the life of the glass.
