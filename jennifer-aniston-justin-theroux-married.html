Details from Jennifer Aniston and Justin Theroux's Wedding

Update, 8/20: Upon returning from her honeymoon in Bora Bora, Jennifer Aniston attended the Los Angeles premiere of She's Funny That Way last night with a sparkly new ring to show. The actress debuted her gold wedding band, designed by Jennifer Meyer and encrusted with diamonds, while walking the red carpet. 

Media Platforms Design Team

While interviewing on the red carpet, Aniston kept her wedding and honeymoon details under wraps, telling ET, "I'm not telling you any of that stuff! We had the beautiful luxury of having a beautiful private moment and I'm going to be selfish and keep it that way!"

Congratulations to the happy newlywed! 

Update, 8/15: While much of Jennifer Aniston and Justin Theroux's private wedding ceremony last week still remains a secret, Orlando Bloom revealed more details from inside the nuptials. In an interview with E! News, Bloom dished on how well Jimmy Kimmel did at officiating the ceremony, "Great! I mean, Jimmy's amazing. He pitched it so perfectly. They're a wonderful couple. I was really honored to be there and it was a great night."

The actor also seems to have developed a bit of a man crush on Howard Stern, who gave a toast to the newlyweds at the ceremony. "I have a man crush on him. Have you seen him? He's the most handsome thing you've ever seen," Bloom said of Stern, "I mean, he thinks I'm handsome! That's ridiculous! He's seeing a reflection of himself—that's what it is." It seems like love certainly was in the air at Aniston and Theroux's wedding. 

Update, 8/11: Even though Jennifer Aniston's Friends co-star, Courtney Cox, was her maid of honor at her wedding to Justin Theroux last week, two of Aniston's other co-stars didn't get the invite. People reports that when asked about the ceremony, Matt LeBlanc and Matthew Perry didn't receive invitations: "I think they're a great couple. I think she's happy. And that's all I care about is that Jen's happy," LeBlanc said. "If she wanted me there, I would have been there."Said Perry, "It was a surprise to me as well. They're a lovely couple, and I'm happy for them." In Aniston's defense, the wedding was very small: just 70 people attended the affair, which was disguised as a birthday party for the bride. 

Update, 8/7: More details from the Aniston-Theroux wedding have emerged. People reports that Courtney Cox and tattoo artist Scott Campbell played the roles of maid of honor and best man, respectively, while Sia performed "Chandelier" following the ceremony. The couple's rings were designed by jewelry designer Jennifer Meyer, who attended the ceremony with husband Toby Maguire. Other guests included Ellen DeGeneres and Portia de Rossi, Will Arnett and Jimmy Kimmel. The newlyweds are set to honeymoon in Bora Bora on Thursday.

Original Post, 8/6: After a three-year engagement, Jennifer Aniston and Justin Theroux married at their Bel Air home yesterday, People reports. The couple tied the knot in front of a crowd of more than 70 guests, including Lisa Kudrow, Emily Blunt and Chelsea Handler, E! reports.

Just last year, Aniston appeared on the cover of BAZAAR and gushed about her then-fiancé: "He's one of the most humble, decent human beings...it's almost impossible to get bored with one another. We've tried so hard!"

Congratulations to the newlyweds!
