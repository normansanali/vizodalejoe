Princess Misty - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube personality and content creator best known for her prank, reaction, and general lifestyle videos. She has amassed more than 550,000 subscribers on her self-titled channel. During quarantine in 2020, she posted her "crashing random people online classes !!! || STARTED DRAMA" video and received over 1.4 million views on it.

Before Fame

One of her earliest uploads was a sour drink challenge.

Trivia
