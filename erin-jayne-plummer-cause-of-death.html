Erin Jayne Plummer Cause Of Death

Erin Jayne Plummer, best known for hosting advertorials on Studio 10 and shopping channel TVSN, died suddenly at the age of 42 during the weekend in May 2022.

On Monday, May 23rd’s broadcast, Sarah Harris and Angela Bishop announced Erin’s death, calling her a “beautiful human inside and out” and a “gold-medal mother” to her children. Her husband and three daughters survive her. Her cause of death was not disclosed

Sarah Harris, who announced Erin’s death on Monday, said: ‘Erin has been a warm and recognisable face on Australian television for two decades, and she’s spent a lot of that time cheering up our mornings with her cheerful attitude and dazzling grin. ‘We’ll miss your sunshine around the studio, Erin Jayne, and we’re all sending love and strength to her family,’ she said. ‘You will be much missed.’

Erin was also a six-year presenter for Aerobics Oz Style. From 1982 until 2005, the instructional aerobics TV series had a global audience of 40 million people and was broadcast in more than 80 countries.

Erin was an exceptional synchronised swimmer who represented Australia at three world championships and won 11 national titles in addition to her TV career.
