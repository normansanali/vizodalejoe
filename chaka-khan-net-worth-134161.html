Chaka Khan Net Worth

Chaka Khan is an iconic R&B vocalist who was born on March 23, 1953 in Naval Station, IL. She is best known for her hit version of Prince's "I Feel for You" and for being the frontwoman for the band Rufus. Her career has spanned over four decades and she is considered one of the most influential female singers of all time.
