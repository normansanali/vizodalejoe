Just How Much Is Sandra Bullock's Net Worth Now?

Being Hollywood's richest and most beautiful actress isn't easy. But Sandra Bullock seems to do it without breaking any sweat! She is a spectacular actress, producer, and philanthropist as well. She has acted in many hit movies such as Gravity, Speed,Heat and Oceans 8 and in 2010 was also Time's 100 most influential people in the world! Being a star who has her name on the Walk of Fame, what do you think her net worth would be now?

Bullock began her career in 1992 with the movie Love Potion No.9, however, the movie wasn't such a hit. Then in 1994, she was cast in Speed and since then, her track record sped off to a great start!

She began performing in movies such as While You Were Sleeping (1995), A Time to Kill (1996), Hope Floats (1998) Miss Congeniality (2000) The Blind Side (2009) to name a few all of which were successful. Bullock also gave her voice in Minions for the character 'Scarlet' which grossed over $1 billion at the box office. Apart from that, she was also given roles in various T.V Shows.

She owned a restaurant called Bess Bistro but in 2015, it was shut down. She then opened Walton's Fancy and Staple and also had been a star endorser for various cosmetic businesses.

The 55-year-old star got divorced in 2010 with the American television personality, Jesse James whereupon she had to move away from the spotlight for quite some time to raise her two children, Louis and Laila.
