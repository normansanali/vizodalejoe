Pelvic Inflammatory Disease

Megan has bravely shared her experience with pelvic inflammatory disease (PID), a condition that affects the female reproductive system. PID occurs when bacteria from the vagina or cervix spreads to the uterus, fallopian tubes, or ovaries, causing inflammation and potential damage. This condition can lead to severe pain, fever, and other uncomfortable symptoms, making it difficult for individuals like Megan to carry out their daily activities.
