Exploring Excision’s Career and Contributions

Jeff Abel, better known as Excision, is not only a music producer and DJ but also a record label owner. With his own labels, Rottun Recordings and Subsidia Records, he has made significant contributions to the electronic music industry.

Excision released his debut studio album, “X Rated,” in 2012, which received critical acclaim. He has also co-founded the dubstep supergroup Destroid and played a major role in producing their tracks.

Excision’s live shows are renowned for their epic scale and immersive experience. He brings enormous sound systems and massive visual displays to complement his intense bass-driven tracks.

In 2020, Excision launched Subsidia Records, a record label that showcases a diverse range of electronic music and supports emerging artists.
