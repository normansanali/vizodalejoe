The Governors Ball Announces 2015 Lineup

Recommended Videos

Following hot on the heels of Coachella’s lineup announcement, The Governors Ball has revealed who will be playing during the 2015 outing of the festival. Returning to Randall’s Island and taking place on June 5th-7th, the organizers have put together an eclectic lineup of talent that is sure to excite New York natives.

Headlining will be deadmau5, Drake, The Black Keys, Florence + The Machine and Lana Del Rey, with support from a ton of other notable acts, including A-Trak, Chromeo, Flume, Vance Joy, Gorgon City and many, many more. It’s a strong mix of indie artists and headliners and the festival as a whole will no doubt rival Coachella, as every year seems to be better than the last.
