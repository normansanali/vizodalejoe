The Rock Congratulates Chris Jericho on Double Or Nothing Sellout

Dwayne ‘The Rock’ Johnson took to Twitter to congratulate Chris Jericho on ‘Double Or Nothing’ being sold out in just four minutes. On All Elite Wrestling’s Twitter page, a video of Jericho was posted where he claims that he is the reason for the sellout. The Rock jokingly says that he loves Jericho’s ‘baby face promos’ and goes on to congratulate Jericho. Cody and Adam Page also had some fun with The Rock’s comments.
