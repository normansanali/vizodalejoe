Margot Robbies Best Red Carpet Fashion Looks

Like a true leading lady, Margot Robbie slays on screen and off. From flowing gowns and silver tones to structured silhouettes and pops of color, the award-winning actress is a force to be reckoned with on the red carpet.

A large part of the Australia native’s fabulous fashion is due to her masterful stylist Kate Young. The celebrity stylist describes Robbie’s style as “cool girl” and “very easy.” The dynamic duo has worked together for years and never fails to command attention with her stylish wardrobe.

“She’s gorgeous and fun and inspiring,” the stylist told Us Weekly in 2019. “She loves clothes and is very engaged with the process, which makes her my ideal kind of client.”

Every new movie premiere brings the chance to experiment with her look. From her white Armani Privé gown at The Wolf of Wall Street premiere in 2013 to her gold Versace number at the 2018 I, Tonya red carpet, Robbie is constantly making a style statement.

For the Once Upon a Time in Hollywood press tour circa 2019, Young wanted to pull out all the stops.

At the Berlin premiere in August 2019, Robbie rocked an olive green Jacquemus gown. The number featured a plunging neckline, an A-line skirt and button details running down the center of the dress. She teamed the ensemble with beachy waves, warm eyeshadow and rosy cheeks.
