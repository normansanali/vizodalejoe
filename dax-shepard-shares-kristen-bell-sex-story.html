Dax Shepard Shares Kristen Bell Sex Story

“Okay, they didn’t walk in while I was, like, pounding mom,”  the El Camino Christmas actor, 42, revealed during a Wednesday, November 8, appearance on Jimmy Kimmel Live! “We put on the TV in the living room — we’re bad parents — and then we stole away for a minute, afternoon delight, but we were just under the sheets and they walked in. It was more just explaining why we’re taking, like, a nap in the afternoon while they were loose.”
