Hurricane Chris Biography

Hurricane Chris is an American rapper (the actual name is Christopher Jerrod Dooley Jr.). His radio debut single was “A Bay Bay Bay” and his debut studio album was 51/50 Ratchet (2006). In June 2020, Chris was arrested for his link to the shooting in Shreveport, La. According to the police, he was charged with a second-degree murder.
