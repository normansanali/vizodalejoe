Christine Brown News - Us Weekly

Name:Christine Brown

Relationship:Separated

Birthday:April 18, 1972

About

Christine Brown is best known for appearing on TLC’s Sister Wives, which follows her life with husband Kody Brown and his three other wives: Meri Brown, Janelle Brown and Robyn Brown.

Christine, who grew up in a polygamous household, is the third of Kody’s wives. They met in 1990 through her sister and hit it off quickly. The pair had their first date on Valentine’s Day in 1994 and Kody popped the question on the spot. Christine and Kody tied the knot that same year.

Together, they have six children: Paedon, Aspyn, Mykelti, Gwendlyn, Ysabel and Truely.

In November 2021, Christine announced via social media that she and Kody split after more than 25 years together. Their separation began playing out during season 17 of the TLC reality show, which premiered in fall 2022.
