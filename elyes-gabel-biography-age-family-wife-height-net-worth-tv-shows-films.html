Elyes Gabel Biography

Elyes Gabel is an English actor well known for his roles in the BBC medical drama Casualty, in the CBS series Scorpion, in Seasons 1 and 2 of the HBO series Game of Thrones, and in the BBC drama Waterloo Road.

Gabel dropped off the drama school and appeared on the children’s TV show I Love Mummy. He ‘d been involved in West Side Story stage productions, Oliver! And The Anne Frank Diary. Gabel joined the cast of Casualty in 2004. In 2007, he decided to leave Casualty to pursue new challenges for the acting and concentrate with his band on recording music.

Gabel has starred alongside Martin Shaw in the BBC drama Apparitions. On 13 November 2008, the first episode aired on BBC One. He continued to appear in Dead Set, a TV horror show. Gabel starred in ITV’s six-part drama Identity. He has played The Borgias in episode 3 of Showtime’s. He appeared in the second Psychoville series in May 2011. He was later cast into the Game of Thrones by HBO.
