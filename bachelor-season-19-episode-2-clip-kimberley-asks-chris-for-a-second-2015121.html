Bachelor Season 19, Episode 2 Clip: Kim Asks Chris for a Second Chance

She’s not taking no for an answer! On the Jan. 5 season premiere of season 19 of The Bachelor, Chris Soules sent home eight women from the group of 30 beautiful contenders for his heart. In the final moments of the episode, though, one of the rejected women marched back into the Bachelor mansion, and appealed to Chris. An exclusive clip for episode two previews a little bit more about the ensuing conversation between Chris and Kimberly, a 28-year-old yoga instructor. 

In the clip, Chris and the girls who received roses are celebrating together with champagne when Kimberly reenters and approaches Chris. “Can I steal you for a second?” she asks, before the pair walk outside.

Inside the house, the remaining girls speculate on how the conversation is going.

“We’re all just kinda wondering at this point — why is this happening? Is this allowed? What is she gonna say, what is she gonna do?” asks Carly, the 29-year-old cruise ship singer from Texas.

Outside, Kimberly is choking back tears as she asks Chris to reconsider her. Talk about relentless!
