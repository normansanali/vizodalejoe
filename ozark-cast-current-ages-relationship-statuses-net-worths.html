'Ozark' Cast: Current Ages, Relationship Statuses, &amp; Net Worths

Netflix's television series Ozark is a chilling drama that stars big-named celebrities including Jason Bateman, Laura Linney, and Julia Garner. Currently, devoted fans are anxiously awaiting the show's fourth and final season to see just how it ends for the Byrde family.

While viewers can rewatch the show's three seasons on Netflix to familiarize themselves with what already happened to Marty and the rest of the family before season four is released, why not take a deeper look at the lives of the actors behind the characters? What are some of the main actors and actresses' real ages, relationship statuses, and who is making the most money in Hollywood?

10 Jason Bateman: Age 52, Married To Amanda Anka, $30 Million

Actor Jason Bateman stars as Marty Byrde in the Netflix series Ozark and for his role, Bateman was nominated for a Golden Globe for Best Actor in a Television Series Drama and won a Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Directing for a Drama Series in 2019 for directing the series.

RELATED: The Cast Of 'Arrested Development': Where Are They Now?

At 52-years-old, Bateman has been in several movies and television shows with his most prominent roles in the show Arrested Development and the film Horrible Bosses, alongside Jennifer Aniston. Bateman is married to Amanda Anka, who is also an actress, and currently has a net worth of $30 million.

9 Laura Linney: Age 57, Married To Marc Schauer, $10 Million

Actress Laura Linney, who is currently 57-years-old, stars as Jason Bateman's wife Wendy Byrde on the Netflix drama. Linney has been in Hollywood for quite some time, starring in the Showtime series, The Big C, which won her her fourth Emmy, and has been in several movies including 2000's You Can Count On Me, which she received an Oscar nomination for.

Linney has been married to her husband Marc Schauer since 2009 and her current net worth is $10 million.

8 Julia Garner: Age 27, Married To Mark Foster, $3 Million

Julia Garner received high praise for her role as Ruth Langmore in Ozark. Garner went on to win two consecutive Primetime Emmy Awards for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series.

Garner is currently 27, and is married to Mark Foster, a musician best known as the lead singer in the group Foster the People. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Garner has a net worth of $3 million.

7 Sofia Hublitz: Age 21, Possibly Dating David Gagliardi, $500,000

21-year-old actress Sofia Hublitz stars as Jason Bateman and Laura Linney's daughter, Charlotte Byrde in the crime drama. According to IMDb, besides working on the fourth season of Ozark, the young actress will be starring as Darla Walker in 2021's action/thriller Ida Red.

Celebrity XYZ reports that Hublitz has been dating musician David Gagliardi, but there hasn't been much mention about their relationship as of this writing. She has a current net worth of $500,000.

6 Lisa Emery: Age 69, Single, $1.9 Million

69-year-old actress Lisa Emery is best known for her role as Darlene Snell in the Netflix show, but theater buffs will recognize the star from several plays including Relatively Speaking, The Women, and The Matchmaker.

Emery was married to actor Josh Pais from 1990 until 2003, but she is currently now single. According to Net Worth Post, Emery has a net worth of $1.9 million.

5 Skylar Gaertner: Age 17, Single, $200,000

17-year-old Skylar Gaertner stars as Jason Bateman and Laura Linney's young son, Jonah Byrde in the series, and looking through his acting credits, Gaertner has been working as an actor since he was four.

Currently, it doesn't appear as if Gaertner is seeing anyone and reportedly has a net worth of $200,000.

4 Janet McTeer: Age 59, Married To Joseph Coleman, $4 Million

Actress Janet McTeer stars as the Navarro drug cartel's attorney Helen Pierce in Ozark and became a series regular after having a recurring role in season 2. The actress is currently 59-years-old and is married to husband Joseph Coleman.

McTeer has won several awards as an actress including a Golden Globe Award for her role in the 1999 film Tumbleweeds. McTeer currently has a net worth of $4 million.

3 Charlie Tahan: Age 22, Single, $1 Million-$5 Million

Charlie Tahan is best known for his role as Wyatt Langmore in the Netflix series, but he has also done other major acting projects including voicing Victor Frankenstein in the animated film Frankenweenie and star as Zac Efron's younger brother in Charlie St. Cloud.

The 22-year-old actor is believed to be currently single and has a net worth between $1 million and $5 million.

2 Peter Mullan: Age 61, Married To Robina Qureshi, $1 Million

Actor Peter Mullan starred as Jacob Snell in the first two seasons of Ozark, but has a lot of upcoming projects in the works after his departure from the series including a role in the upcoming Amazon Prime streaming tv series, The Lord of the Rings and The Underground Railroad.

Mullan is currently 61-years-old and has been married to his wife Robina Qureshi since 2007. He has a net worth of $1 million.

1 Tom Pelphrey: Age 38, Single, $12 Million

Actor Tom Pelphrey stars as Laura Linney's character's brother named Ben Davis who joins the Ozark cast in season three. However, he is also best known for playing the roles of Jonathan Randall and Mick Dante in the CBS series' Guiding Light and As The World Turns.

The 38-year-old star is reportedly single as of this writing and has a net worth of $12 million thanks to his many recognized roles.
