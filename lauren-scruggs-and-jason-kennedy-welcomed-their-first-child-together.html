Lauren Scruggs And Jason Kennedy Welcomed Their First Child Together

Lauren Scruggs, a lifestyle blogger, and her husband, Jason Kennedy, had their first child together on Sunday, April 3. Ryver Rhodes, the couple’s kid, was born, the pair revealed on Instagram. The couple posted a number of photos of their firstborn, including one of the fathers leaning towards the mother as she cuddled their infant.

Another photo showed the new mother posing for the camera while lying down on her arm. The caption on the Instagram photo read:

In an interview with Access Hollywood, the new parents discussed life with their child after the delivery. According to Jason Kennedy:

“It’s impossible to come up with words to adequately digest the previous 48 hours. Seeing my kid for the first time was like something out of a movie. “I continued shutting my eyes till I realized it was true.”

Lauren Scruggs and their child are “doing fantastic,” according to the former entertainment anchor.

Lauren Scruggs rose to prominence.

Scruggs gained a large internet following after her catastrophic aircraft propeller accident in 2011, in which she lost an arm and an eye. In the years afterward, she has spoken openly about the incident and has begun to share her passion for lifestyle and fashion content with her fans. She has 362k Instagram followers as of now. The new mom also founded The Stranded Shop, The LSK Foundation, and The Clean Sweep.

Net worth

Lauren Scruggs has a net worth of $1 million as of September 2023, according to Celebrity Net Worth. She earns the majority of her money from her businesses and brand sponsorships, which she promotes on her Instagram page. Scruggs has important relationships with corporations such as Walmart, Tejari, Bobbie, and others.

She got engaged to her now-husband Jason Kennedy, widely known for his work on E! Networks, in May 2014. The pair tied the knot in December of 2014. When the couple decided to conceive their own offspring in January 2021, they began recording their IVF procedures on social media. Scruggs’ reproductive issues have been discussed openly by the two in the past. When they eventually became pregnant, they announced it to the world with a humorous video on Instagram. When asked about her pregnancy experience, the fashion blogger told People:

“It’s always difficult when a doctor informs you that you can’t have children naturally and you keep running into issues, but we didn’t sit around and mope about it.” We were really fortunate to meet with several incredible physicians and professionals.”

The new mother also shared that the hardest part of her journey had “four alarms to set every day for months taking medicine,” but they now have an “overall sense of peace” because of their “faith in God” and their family and friends who supported them throughout their journey to becoming parents.
