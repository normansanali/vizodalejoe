Carmen Salinas Net Worth

Carmen Salinas is a Mexican actress born on October 5, 1933 in Torreon, Mexico. She is well-known for her roles in films such as Elisa (1979) and Corrupcion (1984), as well as her telenovela roles in Maria Mercedes (early 1990s) and Mundo de Fieras (early 2000s). She has become a beloved figure in Mexican culture for her long career in the entertainment industry.

About

Notable for her roles in films such as Elisa (1979) and Corrupcion (1984), Salinas is perhaps even better known for her telenovela roles in Maria Mercedes (early 1990s) and Mundo de Fieras (early 2000s).

Before Fame

Her earliest acting credits include roles in the 1970s films La vida Inutil de Pito Perez and Dona Macabra.

Trivia

In 2002, she received a special TVyNovelas Award for artistic achievement over the course of her five-decade acting career.

Family Life

She was born in Torreon, Coahuila, Mexico, to Jorge Salinas Perez Tejada and Carmen Lozano Viramontes. Her marriage to Pedro Plascencia lasted from 1956 until his death and produced children named Pedro, Maria, and Jorge.

Associated With

She acted opposite Catherine Fulop on the Venezuelan telenovela Mundo de fieras.
