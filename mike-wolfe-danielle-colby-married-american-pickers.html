Are Mike Wolfe and Danielle from American Pickers married?

AMERICAN Pickers’ Danielle Colby and Mike Wolfe have known each other for various years prior to the show airing on the History Channel.

The pair began working together in 2010 and have been filming the series for 22 seasons so far.

Are Mike Wolfe and Danielle from American Pickers married?

Business partners Mike Wolfe and Danielle Colby are just close friends.

Wolfe developed the idea for American Pickers and asked Colby to be a part of the series as the shop manager for the antique stores.

The pair now star together in the newest season as Wolfe travels in search for more antiques.

Who is Mike Wolfe married to?

Wolfe has been married to Jodi Faeth since 2012.

The pair met back in 1994 and have one daughter together, Charlie Faeth Wolfe.

Charlie was reportedly born with a cleft-lip palate but received surgery and is doing well.

The couple were also public about Faeth’s stage 2 Non Hodgkin's Lymphoma cancer diagnosis in 2013.

She recovered and has been cancer-free for nearly six years.

Who is Danielle married to?

Danielle was previously married to Robert Strong back in 2004.

The couple share two children together: Miles and Memphis.

She reportedly attributed fame and stardom from the show as to reasons why her marriage ended but continued appearing in the series as a regular.

Colby then remarried French artist Alexandre De Meyer in 2015 but split three years later.

She is currently dating Jeremy Scheuch and is reportedly splitting her time between Iowa and Puerto Rico.
