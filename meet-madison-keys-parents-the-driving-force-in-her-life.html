Meet Madison Keys Parents: The Driving Force in Her Life

Madison Keys’ parents are Rick and Christine Keys. The couple have four daughters. Sydney is older than Madison and has two younger sisters named Montana and Hunter.

Madison Keys’ mother

Madison Keys’ mother is Christine Keys, an alumna of Marycrest University where she earned her undergraduate degree.

She pursued a career in law after completing her legal studies at De Pau University. Christine made the selfless decision to leave her career behind and relocate to Florida when Madison was presented with the opportunity to train at the Evert Academy.

This exemplifies the unwavering support and dedication that parents often provide as the steadfast foundation for their children’s success.

What does the couple do for a living?

Both Rick and Christine Keys are lawyers. While Christine retired and relocated with their daughters to Florida for Madison’s coaching, Rick remained in Rock Island, where he serves as a partner at Keys Law Offices. He specializes in handling criminal defense and personal injury cases.
