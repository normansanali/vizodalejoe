Selena Gomez Doesnt Approve Of Taylor Swifts New Romance With Travis Kelce

Highlights

  • Selena Gomez is concerned about Taylor Swift's relationship with NFL star Travis Kelce, questioning his intentions.
  • Selena confronted Taylor about her concerns, wanting her to see things from a different perspective and discuss the romance.
  • Travis Kelce's past, including a reality TV show where he dated 50 women and allegations of cheating, adds to Selena's skepticism.

Taylor Swift has made it known how important her girl squad is, but that doesn’t mean she needs everyone’s approval when it comes to her dating life. According to a new report, not everyone close to the singer is a fan of her relationship with NFL star Travis Kelce, including Selena Gomez.

“Selena is wary of the whole thing. Selena doesn’t want to be mean or negative, but she’s just not sure about Travis,” a source claimed to OK! Magazine.

Selena Is Concerned About Travis’ Intentions With Taylor

The insider further claimed that Selena even confronted Taylor about her concerns last month, though Taylor and Travis’ romance has only appeared to heat up since then.

“Selena is Taylor’s best friend, so she brought up her concerns over dinner. She wants to make sure Taylor is aware of how bonkers this all looks,” they explained. “She wants Taylor to see things from a different perspective and talk to her about how this romance started and where it could possibly go.”

Apparently, Travis’ previous reality television show Catching Kelce is one of the red flags Selena is concerned with. The short-lived 2016 show featured the athlete dating 50 women. It ended with him selecting contestant Maya Benberry to date in the finale, but it wasn’t long before they split amid reports that Travis was unfaithful.

Travis Has Been Accused Of Cheating By Past Girlfriends

Maya recently opened up about their brief romance to stand by her previous claims that Travis cheated on her. She even gave a direct warning to Taylor, suggesting that Travis’ motives for dating her may be questionable.

“The man that I knew him to be when we dated,” Maya told Daily Mail. “Only time will tell but like the saying always goes, once a cheater, always a cheater.”

“Taylor seems like such a fun girl with a beautiful spirit so I wish her the best of luck but I wouldn’t be a girls' girl if I didn’t advise her to be smart!” she went on.

“I’m sure by now she has mastered the ability to see who is really there for her - and who is just using her,” Maya added. “I can only hope that she comes out the teacher in this situation and not the student.”

There’s been speculation that Travis is using his high-profile romance with Taylor for fame and financial game. For instance, his jersey sales have skyrocketed in the wake of his newfound popularity amongst Swifties.
