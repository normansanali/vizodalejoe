Why Lukas Gage Is One Of The Most Popular Young Stars In Hollywood Right Now

Actor Lukas Gage knew that he wanted to be a Hollywood star since he was a kid — Gage went to a movie camp every summer and as a child, he appeared in numerous plays and commercials. In 2013, Gage made his acting debut in the HBO comedy-drama Enlightened, and he hasn't looked back since.

Today, we're taking a closer look at the career of the 27-year-old. Which projects helped Lukas Gage become one of the most promising new names in the industry, and how did a social media post change his career forever? Keep on scrolling to find out!

8 Lukas Gage Will Play A Main Character In Season 4 Of You

The Netflix show You has been a huge hit ever since it hit the streaming service in 2018. While fans are eagerly waiting for the fourth season of the psychological thriller show to premiere — as of this writing a date is still unknown — they can look forward to seeing a new face on the show. While not many details are known about the upcoming season, Netflix did reveal that Lukas Gage will portray a character named Adam. Charlotte Ritchie, Tilly Keeper, Amy-Leigh Hickman, and Ed Speleers will also join the cast for the fourth season of You.

7 Lukas Gage Appeared In Euphoria

In 2019, Lukas Gage could be seen as Tyler Clarkson in four episodes of the HBO show Euphoria. The teen drama is inspired by the Israeli miniseries of the same name, and in February 2022 it got renewed for a third season. Euphoria stars Zendaya, Jacob Elordi, Alexa Demie, Sydney Sweeney, and Maude Apatow — and it currently holds an 8.4 rating on IMDb.

6 Lukas Gage's Infamous Zoom Audition

Lukas Gage was in the spotlight in November 2020 after a clip of him during a Zoom audition went viral. In the video in which Gage is auditioning for a role (he didn't end up getting), the director can be heard saying "These poor people live in these tiny apartments. Like, I'm looking at his background, and he's got his TV and..."

After that Gage interrupted him by saying, "You're unmuted. I know it's a shitty apartment. Give me this job so I can get a better one." Gage posted the recording on his social media.

5 Lukas Gage Appeared In American Vandal

In 2017 Lukas Gage could be seen playing Brandon Galloway in five episodes of the Netflix show American Vandal. The show is a parody of true crime documentaries, and it stars Tyler Alvarez, Griffin Gluck, and Jimmy Tatro. After two seasons, Netflix canceled the show in 2018. American Vandal currently holds an 8.1 rating on IMDb.

4 Lukas Gage Starred In The White Lotus

Lukas Gage's most prominent role so far was certainly in the HBO comedy-drama The White Lotus. In the satirical anthology show, Gage portrays Dillon in six episodes.

Related: Who’s In HBO’s ‘White Lotus’ Season 2 And What Are They Worth?

The White Lotus follows the lives of guests at a tropical resort in Hawaii, and it got renewed for a second season — one which will follow a new group of guests. The HBO show currently has a 7.6 rating on IMDb.

3 Lukas Gage Appeared In Angelyne

Another show that Lukas Gage can be seen in is the 2022 miniseries Angelyne which tells the story of a woman who rose to fame in the '80s after a series of iconic billboards of her appeared in Los Angeles, California. In the Peacock show, Gage plays Max Allen. Apart from the actor, the miniseries also stars Emmy Rossum, Martin Freeman, Hamish Linklater, Philip Ettinger, and Michael Angarano. Angelyne currently holds a 5.7 rating on IMDb.

2 Lukas Gage Is Also Working On Movies

While Lukas Gage is mostly known for his appearances in popular shows, the actor also worked on a couple of movies over the years. In 2015, he got cast in the zombie comedy Scouts Guide to the Zombie Apocalypse, and in 2018 he could be seen in the satirical comedy-thriller Assassination Nation. In 2022, Lukas Gage was part of two movies — the sci-fi rom-com Moonshot and the biopic Midas Man.

1 Lukas Gage Has Plenty Of Industry Friends

Apart from getting roles in famous television and big-screen projects, the talented actor also seems to have plenty of friends in the industry. While the actor is good friends with plenty of his co-stars like Maude Apatow and Hunter Schafer, he seems to be close to famous musicians like Olivia Rodrigo and Billie Eilish's brother Finneas. Knowing people in the industry can certainly help one's career — and Lukas Gage seems to know plenty of famous faces.
