AEW Rampage Spoilers For 2/9 (Taped On 2/7)

After the February 7 episode of AEW Dynamite, All Elite Wrestling taped matches for the February 9 episode of AEW Rampage from Footprint Center in Phoenix, Arizona.

The spoilers, courtesy of the Wrestling Observer via Solo Wrestling, are as follows:

  • The Undisputed Kingdom (Roderick Strong, Matt Taven & Mike Bennett) defeated Best Friends (Trent Beretta, Orange Cassidy & Rocky Romero)
  • The Young Bucks (Matthew & Nicholas Jackson) defeated two enhancement talents (Mondo Rox & Robbie Lit)
  • Mistico defeated Matt Sydal
  • Kris Statlander & Willow Nightingale defeated Ruby Soho & Saraya

WrestleZone will have full coverage of AEW Rampage as it airs on February 9.
