Ed Woodward was born in Chelmsford, Essex, England in November 1971. He graduated from the University of Bristol and worked for PricewaterhouseCoopers and then J.P. Morgan & Co. Woodward advised Malcolm Glazer and his family during their successful takeover of Manchester United in 2005. He joined the team in a financial role before being put in charge of their commercial and media operations in 2007.
