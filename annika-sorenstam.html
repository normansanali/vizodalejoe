Annika Sorenstam

If you enjoy watching golf, Annika Sorenstam is probably not a new name to you. Annika is a well-known former professional golfer from Sweden. As a professional player, she had won 90 international tournaments. Annika competed in nearly 150 events over the course of her sixteen-year career.

Annika Sorenstam was born in Bro Stockholm County on October 9, 1970. She is one of Tom Sorenstam’s and Gunilla Sorenstam’s two children. Her father was an executive member at IBM Office, and her mother worked at a bank. Charlotte, her younger sister, is her only sibling. She is also an accomplished golfer. Annika moved to the United States after graduating from high school and enrolled at the University of Arizona in Tucson.

She won eight LPGA tournaments during the 2001 season. Annika began her ascension up the success ladder. By 2006, she had risen to the top of the Women’s Golf Ranking.
