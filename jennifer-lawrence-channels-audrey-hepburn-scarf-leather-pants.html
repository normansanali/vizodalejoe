Jennifer Lawrence Channels Audrey HepburnWith a Leather Twist

It is officially March, which means a deluge of practical and stylish transitional winter-to-spring wardrobe pieces is inevitably incoming. Leading the way? Jennifer Lawrence’s latest street-style look during Paris Fashion Week.

The No Hard Feelings star was photographed Thursday evening looking casually glamorous in an outfit that blended the best of today’s trends with an iconic Old Hollywood accessory. Specifically, she wrapped her head in a silky champagne, dark-trimmed headscarf—à la Audrey Hepburn or Grace Kelly—tying it off in an elegant knot beneath her chin.

As for the rest of her ensemble, Lawrence updated the classic skirt-suit silhouette with an array of modern pieces, including a pale beige shacket with large black buttons down the center, a long inky-black leather pencil skirt by Matches Fashion’s luxury brand Raey, and glossy croc-embossed knee-high boots with a delicate kitten heel from Toteme. Of course, no outfit is complete without a purse, and Lawrence capped hers off with a buttery black leather handbag from Manu Atelier.

BACKGRID

The actor has been enjoying a packed Fashion Month thus far. In addition to her low-key street-style outing yesterday, she also attended the LVMH Prize cocktail party last night, where she put a sophisticated spin on the red carpet’s reigning sheer trend. She wore a willowy wrap mesh cover-up pinned to the bodice of a speckled dark gray dress, which featured a thin leather belt and draping details across the waist.

Dominique Maitre//Getty Images

And earlier this week, she suited up to sit front row at Dior’s Fall/Winter 2024–25 runway show. This involved a three-piece suit composed of a smoky gray tweed material, paired with a long black coat, sheer stockings, and black velvet pumps.
