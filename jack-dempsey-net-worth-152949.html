Jack Dempsey Net Worth

Jack Dempsey was an American prizefighter born on June 24, 1895 in Colorado. He was known for his aggressive style of boxing, which resulted in forty-nine knockouts. He held the World Heavyweight Title in boxing from 1919 to 1926. Dempsey was one of the most successful boxers of his time, and his legacy continues to this day.
