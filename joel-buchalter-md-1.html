Dr. Joel Buchalter, MD Danbury, CT

Dr. Joel Buchalter is an orthopedic surgeon in Carmel, NY and is affiliated with multiple hospitals in the area, including NYU Langone Orthopedic Hospital, Putnam Hospital Center, The Orthopaedic and Specialty Surgery Center, and Northern Westchester Hospital. He received his medical degree from the NYU Langone School of Medicine, his orthopaedic training at the Hospital for Joint Diseases and has been in practice for 32 years. He specializes in hip & knee reconstructive surgery, including direct anterior approach hip replacement surgery, robotic partial and total knee replacement. He performs same day hip and knee replacements as well as overnight stays. He performs surgery in a new state of the art surgery center with overnight beds. Two nurses to three patients and the anesthesiologist stays overnight to manage the patients.
