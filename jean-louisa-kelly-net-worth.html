Jean Louisa Kelly Net Worth

Jean Louisa Kelly Net Worth

American actress and singer Jean Louisa Kelly has a net worth of $6 million. Kelly is best known for playing Kim Warner on the television sitcom Yes, Dear. She was born on March 9, 1972 in Boylston, Massachusetts, and was raised by a high school English teacher and a piano teacher. Kelly graduated with a B.A. in English in 1994 from Columbia University’s Columbia College.

Kelly has been married to James Pitaro since 1997 and they have two children. Some of her film credits include the 1989 movie Uncle Buck, the 1994 movie Mr. Holland’s Opus, and the 1997 film The Day Lincoln Was Shot. She has also guest starred on several well-known television shows, including Mad About You, Cold Feet, Ally McBeal, Grey’s Anatomy, Ghost Whisperer, and CSI: Miami.
