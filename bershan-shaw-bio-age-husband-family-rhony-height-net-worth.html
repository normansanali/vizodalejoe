Bershan Shaw Biography

Bershan Shaw is a renowned American serial entrepreneur, business and life coach, motivational speaker, author, podcast host, and real estate developer. She owns multiple real estate properties & the CEO Tech Founder of the Urawarrior app. In addition, she founded Warrior Training International (WTI), a leadership and business development organization that helps enterprises to scale their businesses by offering interactive training solutions.

Bershan is America’s #1 Female motivational speaker and has shared the stage with numerous prolific people including Les Brown, Tony Robbins, and Pitbull. She is also known as a two times cancer survivor and is highly acclaimed for her unwavering zest to conquer life and motivate others in the process.

Throughout her career, Shaw has been an advocate for women’s empowerment and has indulged in the upliftment of women across the world with her Unstoppable Warrior Women Movement and the pledge 15 initiative. Moreover, she is a member of the Forbes Coaching Council, Women in Communications, and Women on the Rise organizations.

Bershan is an author of the best-selling book Unstoppable Warrior Woman and Urawarrior:365 Ways to Challenge you to a better life and is the recipient of the BET Honors Trailblazer & Unstoppable Survivor Award. She has also been featured in several media outlets, podcasts, and TV shows including HuffPost, The New York Post, Black Enterprise, Forbes, Yahoo, and others.

Bershan Shaw Education

Shaw enrolled in Syracuse University where she pursued a Bachelor’s in Science and went to New York University to acquire her Masters’s degree in Communication/Business degree. She graduated in 1997 and then later enrolled in Havard Business School in 2020 for a year-long leadership development program.
