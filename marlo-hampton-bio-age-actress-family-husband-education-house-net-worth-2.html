Marlo Hampton Reveals Why “There’s No Place Like Home” at #TheHamptons

Marlo Hampton’s luxe Georgia townhouse, which bears the appropriate nickname “The Hamptons,” offers her a peaceful break from the outside world. The Real Housewives of Atlanta friend has lived there for over a decade and she has really made it her own over the years (take a tour in the video above), and she’s very generous about sharing it with her friends and family by throwing amazing movie nights and dinners.

“The saying is true,” she wrote on her Instagram Stories. “There’s NO place like home. Today I really enjoyed relaxing with my family and friends… just eating, drinking, watching TV and burning my @pozecandle #wasmuchneeded #thehamptons.”

“‘The Hamptons,’ my home, it’s just Marlo,” she told Home & Design. “It’s sexy. You have black flowers, you have black candles. It’s just a groovy, eclectic style. Like, you don’t know what you’re going to get. You’re going to come over here and see a tree branch. You might have a ham hanging over here if I could’ve gotten that blinged-out ham from Barcelona, that would have been amazing. So my home is just all over the place, like me. But it’s amazing. It’s a piece of art.”
