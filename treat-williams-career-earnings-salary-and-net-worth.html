Treat Williams career earnings, salary and net worth

Richard Treat Williams is an American actor, writer, and aviator with over 120 film, stage, and television credits. Williams starred in the television series Everwood from 2002 to 2006 and was nominated for two Screen Actors Guild Awards.

Williams has also received three Golden Globe nominations, two Satellite nominations, and an Independent Spirit Award.

What is Treat Williams net worth?

How much does Treat Williams make a year?

Treat Williams earns $1.5 million yearly.

Williams starred as Dr. Andrew Brown in the WB television series Everwood, which follows a New York City neurosurgeon who relocates his family to Colorado. Although the show’s ratings were never particularly high, it received critical acclaim and had a devoted fan base. Williams received two SAG Award nominations for his role on the show (in 2003 and 2004).

Williams has appeared as David Morton, a friend and potential suitor of Sally Field’s character, in several episodes of the ABC drama Brothers & Sisters. He played Nathaniel Grant in the short-lived TNT series Heartland, which was canceled due to low ratings. He also appeared in Staircase Murders, a Lifetime movie that aired on April 15, 2007.
