See Jennifer Lopez's Layered Corset Top and Hair Bow

Jennifer Lopez is channeling romance with her fashion choices as of late, and I, for one, am here for it. From her gag-worthy Schiaparelli coat made of real white roses to her lavender floor-length cape worn at Ellie Saab Couture's runway show, she's embracing all things sweet and girly.

The latest in her romantic fashion tour? A new Instagram featuring yet another coquette-forward look. The multi-hyphenate, who hit Couture Fashion Week all while promoting her highly anticipated new album This Is Me ... Now, posted to Instagram on Wednesday, Jan. 31, wearing a gorgeous white corset top from Giuseppe Di Morabito expertly layered over an Intimissimi black turtleneck. Her stylist, Mariel Haenn, paired the look with effortlessly baggy matching trousers and a black hair bow, nodding to the "put a bow on it" trend that's everywhere right now.

Her beauty choices also struck the perfect flirty-retro note. Created by her longtime stylist, Chris Appleton, her new bob was pulled back on one side in a bow and flipped up at the ends. As for makeup, J.Lo went glam with boldly lined lips and smoky eyes. She finished the look with a bronzed glow, masterful contour, and a pair of architectural gold hoops.
